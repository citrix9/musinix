About Me

Music Composer and Opera Singer Nicolas J. Chaanine was born and raised in Lebanon. His love for Music and film grew as a child watching great movies and listening to the amazing effect music had on film. He started pursuing a career in composition and scoring for TV programs and radio advertisement at an early age, while maintaining his love for Opera, singing on some of the greatest stages in the world.
